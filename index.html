Art. 161. [human exposure to infection]

§ 1. Who, knowing that he is infected with HIV virus, exposes a different person directly to such a constraint, It is subject to imprisonment from 6 months to 8 years.

§ 2. Who, knowing that he is affected by a venereal or infectious disease, severe incurable or real-threatening life, exposes a direct person to the inflammation of such a disease, It is subject to imprisonment from 3 months to 5 years.

§ 3. If the perpetrator of the act specified in § 2 exposes to the infection of many persons, It is subject to imprisonment from one to 10 years.

§ 4. Chasing the crime specified in § 1 and 2 takes place at the request of the aggrieved party.
